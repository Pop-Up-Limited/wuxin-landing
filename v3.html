Building Tomorrow's Infrastructure

Wuxin Smart Group (五新智能集团) is a leading engineering machinery conglomerate specializing in the design, manufacture, and deployment of advanced infrastructure equipment. From tunnel lining systems to port cranes, our integrated solutions power critical projects worldwide.

Headquartered in China with operations spanning Asia, Africa, South America, and the Middle East, we combine decades of engineering expertise with intelligent manufacturing to deliver precision-built machinery for the world's most demanding environments.
