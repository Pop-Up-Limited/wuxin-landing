About Wuxin

Building the Future of Infrastructure

Headquartered in Hunan, China, Wuxin Smart Group has been at the forefront of engineering machinery innovation for over two decades. We design, manufacture, and deliver integrated solutions for tunnel construction, bridge formwork, mining operations, port logistics, and industrial scaffolding.

Our commitment to R&D excellence is reflected in our 353 patents and a team of 600+ engineers and technicians dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what intelligent construction equipment can achieve.
