About Wuxin

A Leading Engineering
Machinery Conglomerate

Headquartered in Hunan, China, Wuxin Smart Group is a diversified engineering machinery conglomerate with a heritage tracing back over two decades. We provide comprehensive solutions across tunnel construction, bridge engineering, mining, port logistics, and industrial building sectors.

Our mission is to advance mechanization, automation, and intelligence in construction — delivering reliable equipment that matches or exceeds imported machinery standards while serving global markets from Russia to South America.
